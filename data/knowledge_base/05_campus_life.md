# Campus Life

1337 Coding School operates multiple campuses across Morocco, each providing a vibrant learning environment with 24/7 access, collaborative spaces, and a strong community of learners and professionals.

## Campus Locations

1337 has established campuses in three major Moroccan cities:

- **Khouribga**: The original campus, serving the central region
- **Tetouan**: Located in northern Morocco, serving the northern region
- **Benguerir**: Home to the UM6P (Université Mohammed VI Polytechnique) partnership campus

Each campus maintains the same high standards and peer-learning methodology while serving their respective regions.

## 24/7 Campus Access

One of the defining features of 1337 is unrestricted campus access:

- Students can access the campus at any time, day or night
- This flexibility accommodates different learning styles and schedules
- The 24/7 environment fosters a dedicated community of learners
- Security and facilities are maintained around the clock

## Clusters and Coding Pods

The physical learning spaces are designed to facilitate collaboration:

- **Clusters**: Large open areas with multiple workstations where students can work together
- **Coding Pods**: Individual or small-group workspaces for focused, concentrated work
- Spaces are equipped with high-performance computers and necessary development tools
- The layout encourages both collaborative and independent work

## Events and Hackathons

The campus community is vibrant and engaging:

- Regular hackathons bring students together for intensive coding challenges
- Guest speakers and industry professionals share insights and experiences
- Workshops on emerging technologies and best practices
- Social events that build community and camaraderie among students
- Competitions that showcase student skills and foster friendly rivalry

## Alumni Network

1337 maintains a strong connection with its graduates:

- Alumni continue to engage with the school and current students
- Mentorship opportunities connect experienced professionals with students
- Alumni often return to speak about their career paths and experiences
- The network provides job opportunities and professional connections

## Partnerships

1337 has established strategic partnerships that enhance the student experience:

- **UM6P (Université Mohammed VI Polytechnique)**: Academic collaboration and resource sharing
- **OCP (Office Chérifien des Phosphates)**: Industry partnership providing real-world project opportunities and potential employment
- Other technology companies and organizations that support student development

## Community and Culture

The 1337 community is built on values of:

- Peer learning and mutual support
- Continuous improvement and growth mindset
- Diversity and inclusion
- Excellence in technical skills and professional development
- Collaboration over competition, though healthy competition is encouraged

## Support Services

While 1337 emphasizes self-directed learning, support is available:

- Peer mentors and experienced students help newer students
- Technical resources and documentation are readily available
- The Bocal staff provides guidance on academic and personal matters
- Career services help students prepare for professional opportunities
